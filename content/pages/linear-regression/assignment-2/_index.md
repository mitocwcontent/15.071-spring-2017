The consequences of a continued rise in global temperature will be dire. Rising sea levels and an increased frequency of extreme weather events will affect billions of people.</p> <p>In this problem, we will attempt to study the relationship between average global temperature and several other factors.</p> <p>The file <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a href="./resolveuid/3b53e75d1ddb97bd98f40ae5bbee2475">climate_change (CSV)</a></span>&nbsp;contains climate data from May 1983 to December 2008.
